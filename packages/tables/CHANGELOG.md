## [1.0.4] - 2017-12-11
### Fixed (per [#9](https://github.com/zendeskgarden/css-tables/pull/9))

`.c-table__row__cell`.

## [1.0.3] - 2017-12-08
### Fixed (per [#8](https://github.com/zendeskgarden/css-tables/pull/8))

## [1.0.2] - 2017-10-12

### Fixed (per [#4](https://github.com/zendeskgarden/css-tables/pull/4))
