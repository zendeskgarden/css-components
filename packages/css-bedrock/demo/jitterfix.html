Jitterfix

The .u-jitterfix utility is used to reserve space for transitioning (i.e. on hover) to weighted text. Jitterfix depends on data-text attribute content to calculate the necessary transition space.

.u-jitterfix--light [jitterfix]Thin to Light[jitterfix] vs. [jitter]Thin to Light[jitter]
.u-jitterfix--regular [jitterfix]Light to Regular[jitterfix] vs. [jitter]Light to Regular[jitter]
.u-jitterfix--semibold [jitterfix]Regular to Semibold[jitterfix] vs. [jitter]Regular to Semibold[jitter]
